ABOUT US

USMLEInkspire: Igniting Your Path to US Residency

At USMLEInkspire, we envision a future where every IMG and USMG has the tools, mentorship, and experience to confidently match into a US medical residency program. Our goal is not just to help you apply — it’s to help you thrive.

Our Vision: To be the most trusted partner for medical graduates, recognized for integrity, success, and innovation. We provide end-to-end services that position applicants competitively and ethically in the residency match process.

Our Mission: We empower aspiring physicians with refined application documents, polished interview skills, structured guidance, and unique CV-building opportunities like clinical research, volunteering, and observerships.
