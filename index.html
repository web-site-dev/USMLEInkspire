What We Offer

Residency Strategy & Interview Prep

Navigate the NRMP Match process with confidence. We provide personalized guidance on match strategy, program selection, timeline management, and mock interview coaching. Our mock interviews simulate real ERAS interviews, with feedback focused on clarity, confidence, and communication.

Research & Volunteering Mentorship

Elevate your ERAS application with exclusive experiences. We provide a 3-month remote research internship with EhealthyInfo (impacting research experience, publications) and a 2-4 week US-based volunteering placement (enhancing volunteering experience, US healthcare exposure). Further, targeted mentorship connects you to projects like PubMed publications and case reports, building a standout profile.

Clinical Experience & USMLE Advising

We help you plan and secure US clinical experiences, including electives, observerships, and hands-on rotations. We also offer strategic USMLE exam mentorship — including study planning, resource curation, and CCS coaching — to optimize your performance in Step 1, Step 2 CK, and Step 3.

ERAS Application Support

We guide you through every component of the ERAS application — from structuring a strong CV to crafting a compelling Personal Statement. Our tailored feedback ensures your profile reflects your unique strengths and experiences. Whether you're starting fresh or revising, we make your application residency-ready.
